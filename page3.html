EDUCATIONAL STRAND
TVL
The TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood) strand is another academic track in the senior high school curriculum,designed for students who want to develop practical skills that can lead to employment or entrepreneurship immediately after graduation. It focuses on hands-on training in various fields, offering specialized knowledge and certifications that can be used in the workforce.
STEM
The STEM strand (Science, Technology,Engineering,and Mathematics) is an academic track in senior high school that focuses on preparing students for courses and careers in scientific and technical fields.
ABM
The ABM strand(Accountancy, Business,and Management) strand is one of the academic tracks in senior high school,designed for students who are interested in business, finance,and management fields. It focuses on subjects related to accountancy,business principles,economics,and entrepreneurship, preparing students for careers in business management,accounting, or starting their own businesses.
HUMSS
The HUMSS strand(Humanities and Social Sciences) strand is one of the academic tracks in the senior high school curriculum. It focuses on the study of human behavior, society, culture, and the arts. This strand is ideal for students who are interested in understanding people, social issues, and the world around them, as well as those who want to pursue careers in fields like communication, law, education, and the arts.
GAS
The GAS strand(General Academic Strand) this is another academic track in senior high school, which offers a more flexible curriculum for students who are not sure which specific strand (like STEM, HUMSS, or TVL) they want to pursue. It allows students to take subjects from various strands, helping them discover their strengths and interests.
